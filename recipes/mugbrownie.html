Mug Brownie

Quick chocolate craving satisfaction in a cup. Recipe from chocolatecoveredkatie.

Super simple and great for a quick snack when you don't want to risk eating an entire batch of baked goods. The original recipe had variations for plant-based or keto versions.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp + 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp white flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Optional: grease the mug with a bit of butter or oil before beginning if you'd like to remove the browniefrom the mug before eating.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
  3. Microwave until set (will vary, but start with 1 minute, then do 10 second intervals).
  4. Eat!
