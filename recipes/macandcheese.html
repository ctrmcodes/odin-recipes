basic mac and cheese

Warm, comforting, classic. Recipe from allrecipes.

Quick, easy, cheesy. Let's get to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 box (~226g) Elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Cheese, shredded (mix of marble and cheddar)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the elbow macaroni in a large pot of salted water until al dente, ~8 mins.
  2. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a pan on medium heat.
  3. Whisk in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth (~5 mins).
  4. Slowly combine milk while stirring. Cook and stir until mixture is bubbling. Be careful the milk doesn't burn!
  5. Add your cheese and mix until melted (2-4 mins).
  6. Drain the cooked macaroni, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water.
  7. Add the pasta water to your sauce, then mix in the elbow macaroni.
  8. Enjoy!
